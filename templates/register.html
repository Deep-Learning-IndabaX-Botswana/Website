Registration Information

Join us for three days of learning, networking, and collaboration at IndabaX Botswana 2025. Registration includes:

  • Access to all conference sessions and workshops
  • Conference materials and resources
  • Lunch and refreshments during the event
  • Certificate of participation
  • Networking opportunities with speakers and attendees

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there financial support available for students?

Yes, we offer a limited number of scholarships for students. Please email scholarships@indabaxbotswana.org for more information.

Can I register for specific days only?

Yes, single-day registrations are available. Please contact us for pricing and availability.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made before May 15, 2025 will receive a full refund minus a P100 processing fee. No refunds will be issued after this date.

Will the presentations be recorded?

Yes, most sessions will be recorded and made available to registered participants after the event.
