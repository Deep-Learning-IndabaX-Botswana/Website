About IndabaX Botswana

What is IndabaX Botswana?

IndabaX Botswana is a locally organized version of the Deep Learning Indaba, one of Africa's largest gatherings in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Our event aims to strengthen Botswana's AI ecosystem by bringing together researchers, practitioners, policymakers, industry leaders, and students to share knowledge, build capacity, and foster collaboration.

Through workshops, technical sessions, keynotes, and networking opportunities, IndabaX Botswana creates a platform for advancing AI education and application in our local context.

Our Mission

Education

Build AI capacity through accessible, high-quality training and knowledge sharing

Collaboration

Foster partnerships between academia, industry, and government to advance AI in Botswana

Innovation

Inspire and support local AI solutions to address Botswana's unique challenges

Inclusion

Ensure diverse participation and perspectives in AI development
